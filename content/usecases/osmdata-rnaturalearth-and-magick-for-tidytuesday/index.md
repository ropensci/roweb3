#### rOpenSci package or resource used*

[`osmdata`](https://docs.ropensci.org/osmdata), [`rnaturalearth`](https://docs.ropensci.org/rnaturalearth), [`magick`](https://docs.ropensci.org/magick)

#### What did you do? 
I used these packages to create my submission for week 44 of #TidyTuesday, Canadian Wind Turbines.  TidyTuesday is a weekly social visualization activity/project/challenge originating out of the R4DS and larger R community.  `rnaturalearth` provided me with an excellent map of Maritime Canada, `osmdata` for the main highways (using simple features too!) and `magick` for the no-fuss image cropping!
