#### What did you do? 
Used [RSelenium](https://docs.ropensci.org/RSelenium/) to scrape web reviews for Instagram Lite in order to do text analysis in a future blog post.  The package was used to start a remote browser, browse to Google Play, and navigate the semi-infinite scroll and button clicks to load additional reviews.
