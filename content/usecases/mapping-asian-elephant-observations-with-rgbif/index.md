#### rOpenSci package or resource used
[rgbif](https://docs.ropensci.org/rgbif/)

#### What did you do? 
Searched GBIF for observations of *Elephas maximus* (Asian elephant), and used the locations for making two maps: one for the distribution as a heatmap, and one for observations themselves with a popup.
