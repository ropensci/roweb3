#### What did you do? 
In week 20 of #TidyTuesday, a #rstats community exercise around cleaning and visualizing data, I used rcrossref to get the citation counts for Nobel Prize winners (DOI was present in the dataset) and visualized the citation patterns by author and Nobel category (Chemistry, Physics and Medicine)
