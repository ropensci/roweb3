**Join us for 1.5 hours Wednesday June 17, 9:00 Australia Western (01:00 UTC) for
a Celebration of rOpenSci's 15th anniversary, Quinceañera!**

In this virtual mix of Celebration, Networking and Brainstorming, 
we'll be splitting into groups to explore different prompts regarding rOpenSci's past and future. 
Come join the celebration and discuss how you want to see rOpenSci evolving in the future.

This is the second of two such events, the [first](/events/celebrations-2026-06-10) will be Wednesday June 10th at 9am Americas Pacific.
