Join us at Open Source Friday to learn about the rOpenSci Champions Program, an initiative that strengthens open science and research software communities in Latin America! Yanina Bellini Saibene, GitHub Star and Community Manager at rOpenSci, will tell us about the 2026–2027 call for applications, how to participate, and the program's impact on the development of open tools with R, mentoring, technical training, and local leadership. If you're interested in open science, open data, and free software, don't miss this livestream!
