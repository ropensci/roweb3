July 06-09, 2026 [useR! 2026](https://user2026.r-project.org) will take place in Warsaw. 

* [Mark Padgham](/author/mark-padgham) will present ["The (aggregated) history of every CRAN package ever"](https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/433/) on Jul 8, 2026, 1:20 PM.

* [Cynthia Huang](/author/cynthia-huang/) will present ["Reusing 'ggplot2' code: how to design better plot helper functions"](https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/461/) on Jul 7, 2026, 11:10 AM.

* [LLuís Revilla Sancho](/author/lluís-revilla-sancho/) will present ["Maintaining a package on CRAN"](https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/408/) on Jul 7, 2026, 10:50 AM; and ["{teal}: A production-ready modular framework for reproducible data exploration on clinical trials"](https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/401/) on Jul 8, 2026, 10:30 AM.

* [Mauro Loprete](/author/mauro-loprete/) will present the poster ["metasurvey: Reproducible Survey Data Processing with Step Pipelines in R "](https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/497/) on Jul 7, 2026, 5:00 PM.

* [Natalia da Silva](/author/natalia-da-silva/) will present ["An Enhanced Projection Pursuit Tree Classifier with Visual Methods for Assessing Algorithmic Improvements"](https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/453/) on  Jul 8, 2026, 10:50 AM.

* Thierry Onkelinx (git2rdata maintainer) will present [Streamlining reproducible research with `checklist`: new organisation management and enhanced quality control](https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/474/) on Jul 8, 2026, 11:55 AM.

* Katrina Brock (reviewer of distionary) will present [Travel Paths: Bridging the usability gap in open source animal movement algorithms ](https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/462/) on Jul 9, 2026, 10:30 AM.

rOpenSci community manager Yanina Bellini Saibene has served on the conference [program committee](https://user2026.r-project.org/about/organizers.html).
