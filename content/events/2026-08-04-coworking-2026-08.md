**Join us for 2 hours Tuesday August 04, 09:00 Australia Western (01:00 UTC) for [Social Coworking + Office Hours](/blog/2023/06/21/coworking/)**

Come for the full 2 hrs or only as long as you need!

- Cowork on whatever you need to get done!
