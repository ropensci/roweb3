## FADE IN

Nine students from the M.S. in Psychology Research at Brooklyn College are taking the course *Reproducible Psychological Research*, where they are learning how to code for their first time. All of them have their cameras off. They are all learning R for the first time, and as an introduction to R, are being taught using the `mtcars` dataset. Nine **NYC** graduate students, who 

- don't own a car
- don't drive a car
- and have no interest in cars

Even with their screens off, with every additional `mtcars` column talked about, they fell deeper into sleep during the lectures.

> If I wanted my students to learn anything in my class, not only R, I needed to make an immediate pivot.
