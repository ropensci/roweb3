Champions Program

Our program seeks to identify, recognize, and reward leaders in the open science, research software engineering, and R programming communities. It is an amazing platform for developing your open-source project with support from experts. In addition, you will be able to interact, share, and strengthen a network with other people determined to develop open and reproducible science in your place in the world.
